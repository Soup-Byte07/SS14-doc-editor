┬а ┬а ┬а Attack and heal until your MP drops to 3 or lower, then
┬а ┬а ┬а recharge and repeat.

┬а ┬а ┬а Example: Attack, Heal, Attack, Heal, Attack, Heal, MP is at
┬а ┬а ┬а 3, Recharge, Heal, Attack, etc.

┬а ┬а ┬а This works most of the time. If they're at low MP, though,
┬а ┬а ┬а they can't heal, so take advantage of that if you can finish
┬а ┬а ┬а them off.
