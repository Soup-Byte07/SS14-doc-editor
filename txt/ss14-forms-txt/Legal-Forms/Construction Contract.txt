[head=3][head=2]4. [/head]Certificate of Completion[/head]
Work under this agreement shall begin immediately. Upon completion of the Work, the Contractor shall notify the Client that the Work is ready for final inspection and acceptance. The Client shall make payment before the end of the shift unless another payment arrangement has been made.

[head=3][head=2]6. [/head]Laws & Permits[/head]
The Contractor agrees to work in accordance with station laws and regulations. Acquiring a permit to have work performed will be the responsibility of the Client [italic]if a permit IS or BECOMES necessary.[/italic]

[head=3][head=2]7. [/head]Inspection[/head]
The Client shall have the right to inspect the Work being done or that has been done at any time and request that the Contractor promptly correct work that is defective or fails to conform to the agreed Work.

[head=3][head=2]8. [/head]Right to Stop Work[/head]
The Client shall have the right to order the immediate halt of the Contractors work if the Contractor fails to rectify any defective or incorrect work until the cause for such order is eliminated.

[head=3][head=2]9. [/head]Disputes[/head]
Any dispute arising from this Contract is to be solved through mediation. If the dispute cannot be solved through mediation, it will continue to the station court to be settled by a station appointed judge. If a station appointed judge is not available, Central Command may be tasked to provide or appoint a judge. If Central Command is not available, it shall be God's problem.

[head=3][head=2]10. [/head]Governing Law[/head]
This Contract shall be governed under the laws of The NanoTrasen Court of Space.

[head=3][head=2]11. [/head]Severability[/head]
If a court finds that any provision of this Contract is invalid or unenforceable, the remainder of this Contract shall be interpreted so as best to affect the intent of the Parties.

[head=3][head=2]12. [/head]Amendments[/head]
This Contract is not to be amended or modified except by a written agreement signed by all Parties.
